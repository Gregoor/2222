# 2222 in Scratch

Contributed by Ivan Reese ([ivanish.ca](https://ivanish.ca))

To play this version:
1. Download `2222.sb3` to your computer.
2. Go to [https://scratch.mit.edu/projects/editor/](https://scratch.mit.edu/projects/editor/)
3. Click `File` > `Load from your computer` and open `2222.sb3`
4. Click the green flag near the top right.
